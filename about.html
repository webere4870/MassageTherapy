What to Expect

Our goal at RDM Therapy is to provide personalized one-on-one care with myself at each visit. At RDM Therapy we pride ourselves in ease of access to our staff. Information prior to appointment: When you sign up for your first appointment, you will be provided with a link to PTEverywhere to complete specific documents prior to start of care. We are a cash-based PT clinic meaning that we do not bill insurance. Within your PTEverywhere account, you will find a detailed receipt for each visit that you can submit to your insurance for out-of-network.
